The best way to study mathematics is to solve exercises. But it is very difficult to find good exercises. That means being a good teacher takes a lot of time - it is little known, but just preparation for class takes 30% of the teachers' time.

Matemaatik solves this by making it three easy steps - The teacher picks the topic for her next class, gets a list of the best exercises, picks her favourites and can start using them. A repository is not enough as the the teacher doesn't merely need more material, she needs to work with it the easiest way possible. Matemaatik.ee has an easily sortable database of good Math exercises, individually listed and ranked so a Math teacher can easily select, mix and use. Everything on the site is designed so that the teacher can save time, not spend it.

Good preparation is now easier for the math teacher.

  1. Pick a category.
  2. Pick the exercises you need.
  3. Print or share to students.

Key benefits of the app for teachers:

  • Ranking of exercises based on usefulness (determined by an algorithm based on the teachers' interaction with the site - how much they click, print and favourite exercises)
  • All exercises are moderated for minimal quality standards
  • Discussions under the exercises allow teachers to share solutions and reactions from students
  • Lists to save each class and test the teacher prepares with the help of the system

FAQ

Aren't there already many textbooks and online databases where teachers can find problems?
Textbooks are static. Teachers like to put together their own worksheets. Online resources are scarce and all over the place, thus don't work unless a teacher wants to spend a lot of her time collecting and mixing material. A tool that compiles worksheets with few clicks of the mouse button saves that time. And it will attract those teachers that so far did not bother to customize their lesson.
What makes you think teachers actually need this tool?
We have worked together with Math teachers in the whole process of development. In addition to one of our founders being one, we committed few and asked hundreds about the conceptual and practical side of this tool.
